Acrofest this year will feature a few new things that will challenge us to be bold, to work together as one, and to dazzle the minds of young people who have never seen what we do best.

My prayer this year is that Acrofest will be a spiritual revival where you can find a deeper love and relationship with Jesus Christ. That revival will begin with you.

Working together with our schools and universities, our goal is that you will see the awesome power of God at work in your life as we learn why we are here and how God has called us to use our talents.
